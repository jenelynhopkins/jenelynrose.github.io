Clean.Simple.Bold

I build with these 3 things in mind. I want users to see things clearly and not be bombarded with text and pictures that don't flow seamlessly with each scroll. Every interface is simple to use but with features that are bold, stand out, and matter. Based out of Washington State, but originated out of New Mexico. I've been creating since the days of Myspace. HTML, I believe, was every teen's first language without realizing it. For as long as I can remember I wanted to build things, but not just anything, things that would make people happy; so I could be responsible for a little piece of joy in their lives. Programming is my passion and I intend to work in the field for the rest of my career.I am enthralled with the impact that stringing little lines of code together can have on our ever emerging technology and, thus, the future of our society. I can’t wait to see what comes next!

-Jen-
